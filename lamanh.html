Tests Result

For me, each test is a reference, so I can understand myself better. And doing so, it helped me to find out the most suitable way of learning that enabled me to learn everything at 110% of my capacity. Like the second test which shows me which type of learner I am, and through that I know I’m a visual learner. So, I can adapt the way I review my lesson to save time and improve efficiency. The other two tests can show me how I can be more useful in a teamwork environment. And who I can work with to maximize the productivity. But, like a penny, it has a downside, it can disable me the ability to think outside the box, to try new things, to try to work with different people instead of just one type of people. I believed the result can influence my behavior in a team in a positive way. Why because before doing the test, I already consider myself someone extraverted. So, when in a teamwork environment, I can be more confident than ever to work with other teammates as myself, it boosts my confidence when looking for a teammate or making discussion within the team. The test shows me which creative type I am, so it can be a reference for me to know where to look for a new idea. Which eventually saved me time on my project or deadline. But there is more than that, it can bring me passive benefit because the result can become an advertisement for me, which helps people get to know me more, so they can decide to put their trust in me. I should take this into account when forming a team, so I know where my strength is and where my weakness is. Knowing that, I know who to choose to make up for what I lack of and to provide me with more experience in increasing my strength as well as improving my weakness. And knowing that means I can put all irrelevant emotion out of the job and make a logical decision when choosing teammates, so they can work as best as possible.
